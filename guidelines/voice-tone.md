NAU embodies five emotional characteristics:

1. **Welcoming** — Everyone belongs here
2. **Supportive** — We help students succeed
3. **Collaborative** — We work together
4. **Determined** — We are purpose-driven
5. **Respectful** — We honor every person and perspective

## Writing Voice

**Personal over institutional.** Speak directly to the reader. Avoid formal university-speak.

| Instead of | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| "The university provides resources to students..." | "You'll find the resources you need..." |
| "Students are encouraged to..." | "Come explore..." |

**Active voice.** Demonstrate direct relationships; avoid passive constructions.

| Instead of | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| "Students are supported by faculty mentors" | "Faculty mentors guide you every step" |

**Concrete language.** Use specific details and facts rather than abstract metaphors or vague superlatives.

**Concise.** Short sentences. Eliminate unnecessary phrases and complex clauses. One idea per sentence.

## Style Authority

NAU uses **The Associated Press Stylebook** (current edition) as the primary authority for style, grammar, and usage. Exceptions are noted in NAU's own style guide.

Secondary reference: **Merriam-Webster's Collegiate Dictionary** (or any standard American dictionary).

## Formatting Rules

**Dates**
- Write numerals only — no ordinal suffixes
- Correct: "October 3" or "Oct. 3"
- Incorrect: "October 3rd"

**Times**
- Space between number and a.m./p.m.
- Include periods in a.m./p.m.
- Correct: "6 p.m." / "10:30 a.m."

**Phone numbers**
- Format: `xxx-xxx-xxxx`

**URLs**
- All NAU URLs must begin with `nau.edu`
- In print: use friendly URLs with a QR code
- In digital: use clickable hyperlinks, not QR codes (except digital signage)
- Format: `nau.edu/xxxxx`

## Required Legal Language

### Equal Opportunity Statement

Include in all **departmental publications**.

**Short version:**
> NAU is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Institution.

**Full version:**
> Northern Arizona University does not discriminate in access to programs and activities or employment on the basis of sex, race, age, color, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, or protected veteran status.

**For employment postings:**
> Northern Arizona University is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer. Women, minorities, individuals with disabilities, and protected veterans are encouraged to apply.

**Abbreviated (space-limited):**
> EO/AA/women/minorities/disabled/protected veterans
